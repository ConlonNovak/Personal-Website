About


Conlon Novak (he/they) is a designer and consultant with a focus on impact of all kinds — from public policy and societal, to specific and individual.

He attended Carnegie Mellon University from 2016 to 2020, earning a Master of Human-Computer Interaction degree and completing the program’s capstone consulting project alongside his team.

During his undergraduate studies at CMU for his B.S. in Information Systems and Human-Computer Interaction (with a minor in Film and Media Studies), he completed a senior honors thesis, as well as a number of hackathon projects.

Conlon currently works at Deloitte Digital Studios as Studio Staff in the Product Management craft. He has previously worked at accenture and the Software Engineering Institute.

In his free time, he greatly enjoys writing, photography, skiing, tinkering with LEGO bricks, and overanalyzing movies. His abiding love for Denmark will never fade.
